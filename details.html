Detailed Car Information

Cars come in various engine types: gasoline, diesel, electric, and hybrid. Electric cars are gaining popularity due to their environmental benefits and lower operating costs.

Safety features in modern cars include airbags, ABS brakes, stability control, and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) like automatic emergency braking and lane-keeping assist.

The automotive industry is rapidly advancing with autonomous driving technology, which promises to revolutionize transportation.

Maintenance is key to keeping your car running smoothly. Regular oil changes, tire rotations, and inspections are essential.
